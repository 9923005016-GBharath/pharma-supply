# Patient Drug Tracking Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how patients can track their prescription medications through the pharmaceutical supply chain using the blockchain-powered tracking system. The patient portal provides a simple, easy-to-understand view of where your medication is in its journey from manufacture to pharmacy.

---

## Quick Start

### 1. **Login**
- Open the application in your browser
- Click the **"Patient"** button on the login screen
- You will be automatically logged into the Patient Tracking Portal

### 2. **Find Your Batch ID**
Your batch ID can be found:
- On your prescription label
- On the medication packaging
- Provided by your pharmacist

Example format: `ASPIRIN-1762408273270`

### 3. **Track Your Medication**
- Enter your Batch ID in the search box
- Click the **"Track"** button
- View your medication's current stage and journey

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often is tracking updated?
**A**: The system updates in real-time as each supply chain participant records their actions on the blockchain.

### Q: What if my Batch ID doesn't work?
**A**: 
- Double-check the ID for typos
- Verify you're entering the complete Batch ID
- Contact your pharmacy if issues persist

### Q: Why does my medication show a safety alert?
**A**: 
- Alerts are automatically triggered if monitoring detected any irregularities
- Most alerts are resolved before dispensing
- **Always consult your pharmacist** if you see unresolved alerts

### Q: Can I track multiple medications?
**A**: Yes! Each medication has its own Batch ID. You can track each one separately by entering different Batch IDs.

### Q: What if the status hasn't changed in a while?
**A**:
- Some stages (like FDA Review) can take longer
- Manufacturing schedules vary by drug type
- Contact your pharmacy for specific timeline questions

### Q: Is my information private?
**A**: Yes! You only see the supply chain journey. No personal health information is stored on the blockchain.
